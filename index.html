Welcome!

Hi! My name is Anna, and welcome to Curiosity and Wonder! Curiosity and Wonder is my little project to share some of my life and favorite things. I love baking, traveling, and taking photos of whatever I see. The name Curiosity and Wonder comes from my innate curiosity with the world and my wonder at new places I visit or recipes I try. Without further ado, gather your favorite warm beverage and blanket and enjoy looking through my blog!
